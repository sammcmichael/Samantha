Rover Phone Home

In this project we had to determine the location of a rover based on sesnor values. We decided that the rover was in the Marsh Lands because the small deviation in the angular position of the rover told us it was likely in a location with flatter grounds. The constant higher sound told us it was likely slightly noisy where the rover was and the noise was constant. The quick change in light could be caused from the change of night time to day time or from the rover coming out from a shady position. And the constant temperature corresponds with the little temperature changes in the marshlands.
